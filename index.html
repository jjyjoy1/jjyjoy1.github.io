About Me

I am a bioinformatics scientist passionate about unlocking biological insights through NGS data analysis, multi-omics data integration, machine learning, and scalable computational tools. With over 10 years of experience—spanning a Ph.D. in Immunology, postdoctoral training, and industry research—I specialize in translating complex genomic data into actionable discoveries for cancer genomics, precision medicine, and liquid biopsy diagnostics.

My work focuses on:

Liquid Biopsy Innovation Engineering UMI-based cfDNA detection assays and CLIA-certified bioinformatics pipelines for sensitive variant detection in oncology.
High-Throughput NGS Data Analysis Building reproducible workflows (Snakemake/Nextflow) for WGS, single-cell RNA-seq, and proteomics, with optimized cloud/HPC deployments (AWS, SLURM).
Cross-Omics Integration Leveraging VAEs, graph-based ML, and interpretable AI (SHAP, PCA) to unify genomics, proteomics, and immune profiling data.
Biomarker Discovery Developing end-to-end ML/DL pipelines (TensorFlow/PyTorch) to predict drug response, patient survival, and therapeutic subgroups.

I also create open-source tools and interactive platforms (Streamlit, Dash) to democratize data exploration, and collaborate globally to advance computational biology.
